# HexTorq Portfolio 3

HexTorq Portfolio 3 is a long-form cinematic portfolio website for Hextorq. The UI is built like a scrollable brand story where each section behaves like a scene: the company name assembles on the first screen, then the page moves through capability pillars, product examples, process, trust points, and a final call to action.

## Website Experience

The first viewport is centered on the HEXTORQ wordmark with glowing particles, grid details, and a clean startup-style hero message. As the visitor scrolls, the website transitions through full-screen and large-format sections with motion, blur, scale, and reveal effects.

This version is the most narrative of the four. It is not only a list of services; it guides the visitor through what Hextorq is, what the company builds, how the work is organized, and why a client or student would contact the team.

## UI Flow

The visitor experience is structured as a complete brand journey:

1. Hero section with animated HEXTORQ lettering and primary action buttons.
2. Manifesto section with short bold statements about building businesses, workflows, and innovation.
3. Connected-world section using a globe-style visual to show reach and technology depth.
4. Three-pillar section for digital solutions, innovation, and education.
5. Digital solutions section with a dashboard-style business interface.
6. Innovation section showing AI, IoT, SaaS, and automation capabilities.
7. Education and engineering section with a timeline process for student and research projects.
8. Why Hextorq section with trust and decision points.
9. Portfolio section showing selected product-style cards.
10. Process section mapping idea, research, design, development, testing, deployment, and support.
11. Final call-to-action section for starting a project.

## Visual Direction

This version uses a polished SaaS and innovation-lab style:

- Clean dark background with neon gradients and soft glass panels.
- Large cinematic headings that behave like scene titles.
- Scroll transitions that make the page feel guided and premium.
- Product cards, dashboard previews, orbit visuals, and timeline elements.
- A confident, modern interface with more structure than a decorative landing page.

## Best Use

Use this version when the website needs to explain the full HexTorq story in the clearest way. It is strong for a main company website because it covers brand identity, service pillars, education work, product thinking, process, and conversion in one complete flow.

## Content Notes

The primary website UI is in `src/components/hextorq/HextorqSite.tsx`. Update the visible copy there for final company wording, project names, contact links, portfolio examples, and customer-facing claims.

Several brand and portfolio names are currently representative examples. Replace them with real Hextorq products, clients, and contact details before publishing.
